About This Tool

EZ Grader is designed to help teachers, students, and homeschoolers quickly determine test grades. Just enter the total number of questions and how many were missed to get an instant result.

Features

  • Simple input for total questions and incorrect answers
  • Instant percentage and letter grade display
  • Responsive and mobile-friendly
  • No sign-up or login required
